Version 2

Version 2 of the music player will have many new features and will have an overall cleaner design.
We are ditching Tkinter and moving to PyQt6 which is more advanced and offers more customization.

The music player will need to be almost entirely rewritten, so the rewriting process will take some time.
I am also learning PyQt6 in the process.
